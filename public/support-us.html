Ignite Robotics with your support

Support our team in advancing robotics. Your contribution helps the young minds in our programs develop essential engineering skills and gain hands-on experience in robotics.

How to get involved

Donations

Support Ignite Robotics by donating using the link below, or your company’s donation portal
(Benevity ID: 871306813).

Donations go towards sustaining and improving our operations to further support the student community’s exploration of STEM.

Volunteer

Volunteer your time and skills to help make our events a success. As a volunteer, your support is invaluable—whether it's assisting with event setup, managing activities, or providing guidance during competitions.

Sponsorship Connections

If you or someone you know is interested in sponsoring Ignite Robotics, reach out to us and we’ll send over our sponsorship resources!

Corporate Matching

Many large corporations such as Microsoft, Google, GE, and JP Morgan match employee donations at a 1:1 ratio or more for full and part-time employees.

Filling out your employer’s match request form can substantially increase the impact of your contribution! Please let us know if you need assistance with this process!

Volunteer Grants

Companies such as Microsoft, Dell, and Verizon also have volunteer grant programs. Supervising the Robot House after-hours, helping out at events, and other Ignite volunteering opportunities translate into direct contributions from your employer—matched on an hourly rate that they give depending on how many hours you volunteered.

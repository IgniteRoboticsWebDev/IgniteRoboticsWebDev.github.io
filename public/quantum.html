Quantum

High School (9th - 12th)

Overview

Quantum Robotics Division welcomes high school students under 19 by May 1st, particularly those new to the VEX Robotics Competition or transitioning from middle school programs. This division provides a nurturing environment that encourages beginners to develop foundational robotics skills.

This division focuses on providing a comprehensive introduction to robotics, with an emphasis on mentor-led training and development. Students are introduced to essential robotics technologies and team collaboration practices.

Educational Outcomes

Technical Skills

Your student will develop foundational skills in mechanical and electronic engineering, basic programming, sensor technology, and CAD.

Engineering Design

Your student will get an introduction to the engineering design process and effective documentation practices.

Leadership

Your student will develop essential soft skills such as teamwork, communication, and problem-solving.

Schedule

Regular Season

September to March

Early Season

June to August

Post Season

March to May

Teams meet at least twice a week, focusing on guided design and build activities, programming, and maintaining an engineering notebook.

Support and Resources

Each team is paired with dedicated adult mentors, and students have access to structured seminars and clinics to bolster their learning.

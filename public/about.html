About Ignite Robotics

A 501(c) (3) nonprofit organization dedicated to furthering STEM education for future technology leaders.

What We Do

We operate a 6000+ sqft maker-space in Bellevue where students can design, build, and test their robots with professional tools and peer mentorship at their disposal. Our students compete in the VEX Robotics Competition.

We operate three programs: Exothermic, Quantum, and Blaze Robotics at the High School and Middle School levels respectively. With a strong 18+ year history of peer mentorship and alumni connections, we take amateur engineers and turn them into World Champions.
